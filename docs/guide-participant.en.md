# Participant Guide

A guide for those joining an event.

---

## How to Access

Get one of the following from the organizer:

### Join with a Code
1. Go to https://soichirow.github.io/matching-kanri/view.html
2. Enter the 4-character join code (e.g., `A1B2`)
3. Tap "参加する" (Join)

> Full-width, half-width, uppercase, or lowercase characters all work.

### Join via URL
Simply tap the URL shared by the organizer.

### Join via QR Code
Scan the QR code displayed by the organizer using your phone's camera.

---

## Understanding the Screen

### Search for Your Name
Enter your name in the search bar at the top of the screen:
- An **autocomplete list** appears as you type — tap your name (or use arrow keys + Enter)
- A **seat banner** shows your name, table, and opponents at a glance
- Your name is highlighted in purple in the table list
- Your name is remembered for the next time you visit
- If multiple players match your input, the seat banner is hidden — select from the autocomplete list

### Tables Tab
Shows the seating arrangement for all tables. Tables with active matches appear at the top.

### Leaderboard Tab
Displays all players' cumulative scores and rankings. Only visible when score tracking is enabled by the host.

### Match History Tab
Select a player to view their past opponents and the number of times they have played against each.

---

## During a Match (when enabled by the host)

### Check-in
A "来ました" (I'm here) button appears in the seat banner. Tap it to confirm your attendance.

### Self-Score Entry
A score input field appears on your table card. Enter your score and it will be automatically sent to the host.

### Match End Report
A "対戦終了を報告" (Report match end) button appears on your table card. When 2 or more players report, the host is notified.

### Round Timer
A countdown timer appears in the header. It turns yellow under 5 minutes and red under 1 minute.

---

## Auto-Refresh

The screen **updates automatically** (a blinking green dot indicates an active connection).
When the organizer confirms a matchup, it is reflected on your screen right away.

To manually refresh, tap the refresh button in the top-right corner.

---

## Theme Toggle

If the screen is hard to read, use the sun/moon icon in the top-right corner to switch between dark and light themes.

---

## FAQ

**Q: I can't find my name.**
A: You don't need to match the exact name the organizer registered. Just enter part of your name to search.

**Q: The tables still say "empty."**
A: The organizer hasn't run the matching yet. Please wait a moment.

**Q: The screen isn't updating.**
A: Tap the refresh button in the top-right corner. If it still doesn't update, reload the page (pull-to-refresh).

**Q: I want to join a different event.**
A: Navigate directly to `view.html` in the browser's address bar to return to the code entry screen.
